Rock!

Come to our play area to unwind and have some fun. Our play area is open 24 hours and has all the console, arcade, LAN, VR, and tabletop gaming your little gamer heart desires. You can also take a break and play with Legos or gather your friends up and take a turn in our Escape Room. And stay tuned for new test chambers!

Knight of the Round

An Indiana-based, four piece metal outfit, KotR takes Final Fantasy homage to an extreme. Summon them to assault your party with thunderous bass licks, aggressive guitar riffs, and powerful, pounding drum beats.

Smoochieface

Smoochyface is an indie band based in Atlanta, GA. Incorporating narrative elements from video games, cartoons, & comic books, Smoochyface combines the churn of fuzzy guitars with live improvisation for a unique experience in geek rock.

Crunk Witch

Crunk Witch is the musical persona of married couple Brandon Miles and Hannah Colleen. Their music blends pop, rock, and various electronic genres into a cosmic entity of sound. Many of their lyrics are set in a science fiction reality and involve themes of adventure and romance.

Viking Guitar

By day, Erik Peabody is a music mixing master. He is wizard in his trade, but a berserker in his passion. Cue Viking Guitar, his VGM metal outlet where he wields a mighty axe with destructive power. Viking Guitar slays the stage with a fearful presence, striking all who would stand against the Viking gods down through savage and downright reckless aural force.

Cowabunga Pizza Time

Back from the sewers, Cowabunga Pizza Time returns to MAGLabs armed to the teeth with high kicks, fast licks, denim vests, and ninja tricks. These Baltimore based ninja warriors could not be more excited to come party with us after a recent tear of epic performances at MAGStock and Bit Gen, and have plans to knock your socks off with their special brand gooey cheese covered ninja rock n' roll sprinkled with a dash of nerdy nuance. Grab your best denim vest, toss on some shades, and don your headband and come party with CBPT-- oh and skip dinner; these ninjas give out free pizza at every show!

Master Sword

Hailing from Maryland, Master Sword is a 5-piece heavy metal band inspired by music from The Legend of Zelda series. Taking influence from an array of hard rock to progressive metal favorites, the band crafts catchy original music with the melodies you all know and love. Their full-length album, Shadow and Steel, dropped at the beginning of 2018 and is a powerful tribute that any LoZ fan can appreciate.

Kenzie Black

Kenzie Black was first known in the EDM scene as a videographer; In less than two years she has become an international DJ and producer, inspired by やみ かわいい (Yami Kawaii Fashion) and Japanese music. She mixes dark and cute bass house, dubstep, and trap beats with video game and anime remixes in a new, refreshing way.

Flabbercasters

Flabbercasters are masters in the rare art of wizard party rock, and they're bringing their magic to close the stage out Friday night! They just dropped their new album last month, which you can check out at the link below.

LOVE SPREAD

LOVE SPREAD is an electronic noise rock/Post J-POP project based out of Brooklyn, NY formed by Japanese artists Ryota and Narumi. Through a blend of distorted 8-bit chip sounds, noisy guitar tones and infectious J-pop influenced melodies, the duo have been winning the hearts and allegiances of listeners to get trapped in their sonic vortex. They have exploded onto the New York DIY scene off the strength of early series demos and extremely energetic/chaotic live shows both in and out of the city.

Thrillkiller

Exploding on the scene in fall of 2015, mixing elements of metal, rock, pop and others, Thrillkiller delivers a viciously unique style of music. In 2017 and 2018 the band created two back-to-back cinematic music videos alongside their upcoming EP, San Francisco Moto. After the success of their debut music video, The King of 1984, Thrillkiller was back to work on new releases and a follow up video based off of their new single.

Chipocrite

Chipocrite uses original Nintendo Game Boys, often accompanied by guitar, bass, drums and/or other lo-fi sequencers, to create complex audio masterpieces. His multifaceted but catchy compositions combine the nostalgic game sounds of his childhood with modern music influences. He's also the creator and instructor for Soundfly's "Chiptune Crash Course," an online music education series (It's in the link at the bottom!). Chipocrite is currently working on the soundtrack for "EarthNight" a rogue-like runner game coming soon to PS4 and other platforms.

Steel Samurai

Steel Samurai has since December 2013 dug up the most ancient, obscure video game compositions and re-forged them into brutal-yet-richly-textured power-rock instrumentals with delicately layered guitar harmonies, jazzy organ riffs and skull-pounding rhythms. From Wolfchild to Dick Wolf's theme song, Steel Samurai promises something for the whole family, provided the whole family is a bunch of freakin' nerds.

Test Chambers

Test Chambers are the heart and soul of MAGLabs. If you have an idea that you would like to try to make happen, we have the volunteers, space and resources to help bring your idea to life. Our lego room, escape room, VR area, and lock picking have all started as test chambers. The next idea could be yours.
